## AI, Machine Learning, and Data Science Resources

This document provides a curated list of resources for learning about AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, and related fields.

### 1. Core Courses

This section organizes courses by major subject areas.

#### 1.1. Deep Learning

*   **Comprehensive Deep Learning:**
    *   [Mitesh Khapra's Deep Learning](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLEAYkSg4uSQ1r-2XrJ_GBzzS6I-f8yfRU) (YouTube) - Excellent, in-depth series.
    *   [Yann LeCun's Deep Learning (NYU CDS)](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLgF7i4LH-YxacgG0OPmTYe1UUQAvcw9Ke) (YouTube) - From a leading figure in DL.
    *   [Stanford CS230: Deep Learning](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL3FW7Lu3i5JvHM8ljYj-zLfQRF3EO8sYv) (YouTube) - A classic Stanford course.
    *   [fast.ai](https://course.fast.ai/) - Practical, code-first approach to deep learning.
    *   [DeepLearning.AI Specialization (Coursera)](https://www.coursera.org/specializations/deep-learning) - Andrew Ng's popular specialization (some parts require subscription).

*   **Specialized Deep Learning:**
    *   [Mitesh Khapra's RNN with DL](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLGP2q2bIgaNy9_tR-a0fElXJ1Ou-iB2IU) (YouTube) - Focuses on Recurrent Neural Networks.
    *   [Full Stack Deep Learning](https://fullstackdeeplearning.com/) - Covers not just models, but also deployment and infrastructure.

### 4.  Further Reading and Resources

*   **PapersWithCode:** [Papers With Code](https://paperswithcode.com/) -  Find research papers with accompanying code.
*   **arXiv:** [arXiv.org](https://arxiv.org/) -  Preprint server for research papers.
*   **Distill.pub:** [Distill](https://distill.pub/) -  High-quality, interactive explanations of ML concepts (though it's no longer actively publishing).
*   **Two Minute Papers:** [Two Minute Papers (YouTube)](https://www.youtube.com/c/K%C3%A1rolyZsolnai) - Summarizes research papers concisely.
